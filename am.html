What is Affliate Marketing? Easiest way to understand.For begginers.

In today's era, everyone wants to work less than that and can earn money sitting at home. In today's time, there are many options from which we can earn money from home. One of them is Affiliate Marketing.

Q.1 What is Affiliate Marketing?

basically, in Affiliate marketing, we have to promote merchant's products to our blog or affiliate site and if any buyer buys that product from our affiliate link we get the commission.

you have to choose a profitable niche to promote.

Q.2 What is Niche?

Niche is the main topic of your affiliate site. like if you are choosing a DSLR camera then DSLR Camera will be your niche.
in simple language, the niche is that product category you have to promote and sell.


Q.3 How Can I choose A profitable Niche?

choosing a profitable niche is very important for the highest-earning. now how to choose.
First, choose a category like Electronics, Jewellaries, Mobile, where the commission rate is high. or you can promote them easily.
now How to choose a category where the commission rate is high.
all affiliate network have their commission chart where they show everything briefly.
you have to search on your search engine "brand name + Affiliate Commission" like "amazon affiliate commission".




Q.4 How is earnig?

Earning is based on how many people have bought that product from your special link.
a simple calculation of how you can earn and how much if you promoted a product that price is 1000 and the commission rate is 4% and the minimum 100 buyer buys that product then your earning will be:
1000's 4% is 40x 100=4000.


Q.5 How can i join Affiliate Marketing?

Now you maybe thinking about can I can start affiliate marketing.
Now see, who can start affiliate marketing.
You don't need to do any special course to start affiliate marketing or don't need any special degree or qualification.
Anyone can start affiliate marketing.
Joining Affiliate marketing is very easy. the all you need to a laptop and an internet connection. all you have to do create a website and add your product and special link there then you have to choose a brand there are many companies they run their affiliate program As you are begginer i recommend you to choose amazon.com because you all know amazon.com is the biggest e-commerce company. and joining the amazon affiliate program is very easy.


step 1. Open you favorait browser and search "amazon.in" and click to search.
step 2. Scroll down to the bottom.
step 3.Click on Become An Affiliate.

step 4. Click on join now for free.

step 5. Log In or create new account.
step 6. Fill details.

step 7 . Fill Website's URL.
step 8.Fill Your Details for Profile.
step 9.Now You are an Affiliate.
