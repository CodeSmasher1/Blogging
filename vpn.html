Q.What is a VPN?



A VPN (virtual private network) provide us the secure connection between two networks over the internet. We can access the websites or other activities which we are not allowed to access. It also protects your device’s browsing activities and if you are using any public wifi by which you might get hacked, it also protects you from that.

It can hide your actual location and identity over the internet, by that you will be anonymous to the internet.

If you are living in a country and you want access the data or information of other country you can also do that with the help of VPN. That time VPN can hide your actual location.

Actually VPN was created for business purpose.

Why VPN is important?



VPN provides us security when we are surfing the internet. If you want that the data or information you share or retrieve over the internet should be safe and secure than you should definitely use VPN.





Why we dont have to use VPN?


You must have a question in your mind that if VPN is so much important then why don’t everybody use it ?
Then the answer is that there many types of VPNs in which some are free which are public and some are of course private.
Public VPN are free and they are less secure and they also show lots of ads while surfing the internet and it also slows down your browsing speed .
If you buy a private VPN then of course it is so much costly but it is secure, so always remember there is nothing is free !
So if you are so much private person and don’t want your data to be leaked then you must buy a VPN.
